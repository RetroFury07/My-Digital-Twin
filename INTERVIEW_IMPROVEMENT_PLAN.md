## üìù Interview Question Practice Guide

### Top 10 Questions to Master

1. **"Walk me through your proctoring system project."**
   - Practice 2-minute technical overview  
   - Have 5-minute deep dive ready  
   - Prepare architecture diagram  

2. **"Why are you applying for this role?"**
   - Research company/role thoroughly  
   - Connect your skills to their needs  
   - Show long-term interest  

3. **"What's your biggest technical achievement?"**
   - 10 FPS ‚Üí 30 FPS optimization story  
   - Use STAR format with quantified results  

4. **"Tell me about a time you failed."**
   - 40% false positives story  
   - Emphasize learning and growth  
   - Show problem-solving process  

5. **"Where do you see yourself in 3-5 years?"**
   - "Leading AI/ML development teams"  
   - Align with company's AI roadmap  
   - Show ambition + loyalty  

6. **"What are your salary expectations?"**
   - Research role-specific ranges  
   - State realistic range with flexibility  
   - Emphasize learning over money  

7. **"Why computer vision over other AI areas?"**
   - Personal story (what sparked interest)  
   - Technical challenges you enjoy  
   - Real-world impact examples  

8. **"How do you stay current with AI trends?"**
   - Papers you read, communities you follow  
   - Side projects with new technologies  
   - Continuous learning examples  

9. **"What's your approach to debugging ML models?"**
   - Systematic process (confusion matrix, per-class analysis)  
   - Tools you use (W&B, TensorBoard)  
- Specific example from proctoring system  

10. **"Do you have experience with [skill from job posting]?"**
    - If yes: Provide specific example  
    - If no: "Not yet, but I learn fast - here's how I'd approach it"  
    - If learning: Show progress and timeline
